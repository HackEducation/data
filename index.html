As part of my research, I track many aspects of the education technology industry. My data is freely and openly available and is hosted on GitHub. This means you are welcome to download or fork each repository, as well as to contribute feedback or file issues. (Clearly some of these data sets are more complete than others. Don't complain about it. Make an update.)

Hack Education Data Sets


  • acquisitions (data about education technology acquisitions since 2015)
  • alec (data about organizations that are or have been members of the American Legislative Exchange Council)
  • andreessen-horowitz (data about Andreessen Horowitz's education investments, its investors, and its staff)
  • blackboard (data about Blackboard, including investors, investments, and staff past and present)
  • chan-zuckerberg-initiative (investments of the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, as well as staff and executives at the company)
  • deadpool (data about ed-tech companies that have gone out of business since 2013)
  • department-of-education (data about staff, past and present, at the US Department of Education)
  • devos (data about US Secretary of Education Betsy DeVos's investments)
  • digital-promise (data about the people, past and present, involved in Digital Promise)
  • edsurge (data about the ed-tech company Edsurge, including investors and staff past and present)
  • emerson-collective (data about the Emerson Collective's staffing and investments)
  • entangled (data about the people and investors at Entangled Solutions)
  • foundations (data about foundations that are active in education policy and grant-making)
  • gates-foundation (all Gates Foundation education grants since 1998)
  • gates-foundation-people (Gates Foundation staff and executives, past and present)
  • gsv (data on GSV's staff, investments, and investors)
  • horizon-report-higher-ed (historical data about the trends the Horizon Report has predicted for higher education)
  • horizon-report-k12 (historical data about the trends the Horizon Report has predicted for K-12)
  • investments (data about all venture capital deals in ed-tech since 2015)
  • investors (all investors in ed-tech since 2010)
  • ipos (data about education companies that have gone public since 2015)
  • k12inc (data about K12 Inc, including investors, investments, and staff past and present)
  • kaplan (data about Kaplan Inc, including investors, investments, and staff past and present)
  • lds (educators and education technologists who are members of the Church of Latter Day Saints)
  • learn-capital (data about Learn Capital's staff, investments, and investors)
  • mergers (data about education technology mergers since 2015)
  • mit (data about people, past and present, with connections to MIT)
  • new-markets (data about the people and investments of New Markets Venture Partners)
  • nsvf (data about NewSchools Venture Funds' investments, investors, and staff)
  • omidyar-network (data about the Omidyar Network's staff, investors, and investments)
  • owl-ventures (data about Owl Ventures' staff, investments, and investors)
  • paypal (data about the PayPal Mafia)
  • pearson (data about Pearson's staff, investments, and acquisitions)
  • princeton-review (data about The Princeton Review, including investors, investments, and staff past and present)
  • reach-capital (data about Reach Capital's staff, investors, and investments)
  • rethink-education (data about Rethink Education's staff, investors, and investments)
  • spinoffs (data about education technology companies' spinoffs)
  • stanford (data about people, past and present, with connections to Stanford)
  • thiel (data about Peter Thiel's investments, investors, and staff at his firms)
  • think-tanks (data about think tanks that are active in education policy)
  • university-ventures (data about the people and investments of University Ventures)
  • y-combinator (data about Y Combinator's staff, investors, and investments)
